Website Policies

Clear, simple terms for browsing and ordering online.

This page explains how Pizza Uno Sunderland handles website use, online orders and cookies. We've kept it easy to read so customers can quickly understand what to expect before placing an order.

Quick Summary

  • Use accurate details when placing orders.
  • Prices and offers can change without notice.
  • Cookies help remember basket activity and site preferences.
  • You can contact us if you need help with an order or request.

Terms & Conditions

By using the Pizza Uno Sunderland website, you agree to use it for lawful browsing, account access and food ordering. When submitting an order, you should provide accurate contact, address and payment details so we can prepare your food correctly and deliver it to the right place.

We aim to keep all menu items, prices and promotions up to date, but availability may change during busy periods. We may update delivery charges, meal deals or site content when needed, and continued use of the website means you accept those updates.

Ordering & Payments

Orders are only confirmed once they have been received and accepted by Pizza Uno Sunderland. Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on demand, weather or local traffic.

If there is an issue with stock, pricing or delivery coverage, we may contact you to offer an alternative or cancel the order. Please review your basket carefully before checkout.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and help the website function smoothly. This includes remembering your basket, supporting checkout and understanding how visitors use the site so we can keep improving it.

By selecting "Accept" on the cookie banner, you allow us to store cookies used for essential website features and convenience. You can clear cookies from your browser at any time if you prefer not to keep them saved.

Privacy & Contact

Any information you submit through forms or checkout should only be used to manage your order, respond to enquiries and improve customer service. We do not ask for more information than we need to support your visit.

If you have questions about these terms or need support with an order, please use our contact page or call the shop directly.
